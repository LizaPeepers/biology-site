General Biology Course

The word "biology" means the study of life. There are many topics within biology. Below is a list of all of the major topics found within a general biology course:

  • The Scientific Method
  • Organic Chemistry
  • Enzymes
  • Cells
  • Human Body Systems
  • Reproduction
  • Genetics
  • Evolution
  • Ecology
  • Human Impact on the Environment

It has been my personal experience that biology should be taught in the following order, to maximize student understanding:

  1. The Scientific Method
  2. Organic Chemistry
  3. Enzymes
  4. Cells(both plant and animal)
  5. Human Body Systems
  6. Reproduction
  7. Mitosis and Meoisis
  8. Genetics
  9. Evolution
  10. Ecology
  11. Human Impact on the Environment
